Best Exercises for your body

If your goal is to lose weight, build lean muscle mass, or increase your overall physical condition, you need to make sure that you train all muscles groups consistenly.

Here are the best exercises for every single muscle group.

Chest
  • Push Ups
  • Barbell Chest Press
  • Dumbell Chest Flies
  • Chest Dips
  • Dumbell Incline Press
Shoulders
  • Overhead Press
  • Side Lateral Raise
  • Cable Lateral Raise
  • Dumbell Shoulder Press
  • Arnold Press
Arms
  • Hammer Curls
  • Bicep Curls
  • Tricep Dips
  • Diamond Push Ups
  • Cable Bicep Curls
Back
  • Pull Ups
  • One Arm Dumbell Row
  • Deadlift
  • Barbell Rows
  • Cable Pull Downs
Legs
  • Barbell Squats
  • Lunges
  • Leg Press
  • Leg Curls
  • Leg Extensions
Abs
  • Plank
  • Hanging Leg Raises
  • Russian Twists
  • Bicycle Crunches
  • Hollow Hold
